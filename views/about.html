Computer Blocks



What is Computer Blocks?

It is based on a grid and revolves around cables, inverters and source blocks which when used together can theoretically produce any digital program or computer. Computerblocks is a passion project started by a pair of high school students from Melbourne, Australia. We started this as a fun little thing to do but quickly our heads filled with new ideas and ambition. We hope that someday this could be used by many people for both educational and hobbyist purposes.
